What is an RER?

The Resting Energy Rate is the minimum amount of calories {{ name }} needs in order to maintain {% if sex == 3 or sex == 4 %} {{ possessive_pronoun }} {% else %} {{ object_pronoun }} {% endif %} metabolic processes. {{ name }} weighs {{ weight }} {{ units }} ({{ converted_weight }} {{ converted_weight_units }}).

Why is the RER calculated?

Calculating the Resting Energy Rate is a recognized, reliable first step to find how many calories a pet should eat per day. However, every pet is an individual. While this formula works well for 50% of pets, 25% may require more food, while 25% require less food.
