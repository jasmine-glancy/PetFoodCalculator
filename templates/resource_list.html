Nutrition Resources

In a world full of information, it's easy to get lost in the weeds. We've compiled a list of resources to help guide you on your journey to understanding your pet's needs better.

World Small Animal Veterinary Association
Sets the standard for veterinary nutrition guidelines throughout the world. Their goal is to advance the health and welfare of companion animals.
Pet Food Manufacturer Evaluation Report
Pet Nutrition Alliance is a compilation of nine veterinary organizations that offer nutrition resources and education. This report contains information about several pet food companies.

American Feed Control Officials
An independant organization that guides state, federal, and international standards for pet food and livestock feed.
What is a Board Certified Veterinary Nutritionist?
Petfoodology is a blog run by the Nutrition Department of Tufts Cummings School of Veterinary Medicine. This article discusses what a Board Certified Veterinary Nutritionist is, why they are important, and how you can find one.
Interpreting Nutrition Labels (PDF)
A PDF to help you interpret pet food labels from foods found in North America.

Interpreting Nutrition Labels (PDF)
A PDF to help you interpret pet food labels from foods found in the EU.
